Tour to Rajgad


The fort has stood witness to many significant historic events including the birth of Chhatrapati Shivaji’s son “Rajaram Chhatrapati”
The Rajgad Fort was also one of the 17 forts that Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj kept,
when he signed the Treaty of Purandar in 1665,
with the Mughal general Jai Singh,
leader of the Mughal forces.
